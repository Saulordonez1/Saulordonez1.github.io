## 5. Design 
<!-- 
Include basic design and user experience considerations. You can include color schemes, fonts, layouts, etc.
-->
- **Brand aesthetic** Minimal, modern aesthetic with clean typography and ample space with imagery.  
- **Color Scheme:** Modernized Nature color accent: #DEFADE, #4D704D, #171313, #CFA5A5, #E8B7A2
- **Typography:** Crimson Pro for headings, Alata (sans-serif) for body text.  
- **Navigation:** Top menu bar with links to Menus, but in phone layout (with dropdown for the secctions of 2D art and Animation, etc)
- **Layout:** Simple, single-column scrolling pages with sections clearly separated.  
- **Images:** High-quality photos of My art, my self and animations that are interactible.
